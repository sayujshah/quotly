# Privacy Policy for Quotly

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Quotly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, password (hashed), business name, trade type, location (city, state)
- **Quote Data**: Job descriptions, customer information (optional), quotes generated, parts and pricing information
- **Profile Settings**: Default labor rates, tax preferences

### Information Automatically Collected

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns, features accessed, error logs
- **Advertising Data**: Ad interaction data (via Google AdMob)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Services**: Generate AI-powered quotes, store and manage your quotes
2. **Improve Services**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality
3. **Communicate**: Send important updates, respond to support requests
4. **Advertising**: Display relevant ads (via Google AdMob) to support free app access
5. **Compliance**: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms

## AI and Data Processing

### OpenAI Integration

- **Purpose**: Quote generation uses OpenAI GPT-4o-mini to analyze job descriptions and generate estimates
- **Data Sent**: Job descriptions, trade type, location, labor rates (no personal customer data unless you provide it)
- **Data Retention**: OpenAI processes data according to their privacy policy. We do not store your prompts or AI responses beyond what's needed for quote generation
- **Disclosure**: Quotes are AI-generated estimates. Final pricing may vary.

### Data Storage

- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL database) stores your account data and quotes
- **Location**: Data is stored in secure cloud infrastructure
- **Encryption**: Data in transit uses TLS/SSL encryption

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob

- **Purpose**: Display advertisements to support free app access
- **Data Collection**: AdMob may collect device identifiers, location (general), and ad interaction data
- **Consent**: We use Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request consent for personalized ads (GDPR/CCPA compliance)
- **Opt-Out**: You can opt out of personalized ads in app settings or via AdMob settings

### Supabase

- **Purpose**: Backend database and authentication services
- **Data**: Account information, quotes, and app data
- **Security**: Supabase implements industry-standard security measures

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **NOT** sell your personal data.

We may share information only in these circumstances:

1. **Service Providers**: Trusted third parties who assist in app operations (Supabase, OpenAI, Google AdMob)
2. **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights
3. **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability**: Request data in a portable format
- **Opt-Out**: Opt out of personalized advertising

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@shahlabs.org

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
- Secure authentication (Supabase Auth)
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Quotes**: Retained until you delete them or close your account
- **Logs**: Retained for up to 90 days for troubleshooting

## Children's Privacy

Quotly is intended for contractors and business professionals (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via:

- In-app notification
- Email (if provided)
- Updated "Last Updated" date

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

**Email**: support@shahlabs.org  

## Consent

By using Quotly, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.
